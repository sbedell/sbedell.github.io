Net Neutrality

If you support net neutrality and sensible "cyber" laws, consider donating or signing petitions on eff.org. EFF's efforts stretch far beyond just net neutrality; they also support the end of warrantless wiretapping of United States citizens by the NSA, as well as supporting coder's and blogger's rights.

Global Warming

As a skiier, the lack of good snowfall and general decreased winter time in Ohio is disheartening. Go green.

What can you do to help?

Recycle, buy organic, opt for bike or public transit instead of a car if possible, conserve electricity, conserve water. The list goes on and on.
