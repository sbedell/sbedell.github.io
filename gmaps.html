City Locations




Did you know that many cities share their name with other cities all around the world? This is especially true in the USA. Searching for cities via the search box above will give you up to 10 top results for that city name around the world. Try it out by searching for "Florence", "Columbus", "Cleveland", or "Springfield". You might be surprised with what you learn!

Driving directions between the top 2 results are also displayed.

Additionally, click anywhere on the map to show that location's latitude and longitude.
